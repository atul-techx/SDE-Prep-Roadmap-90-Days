Frequently Asked Questions

Is this challenge really free?

Yes, the 90-day challenge and all its structured content are completely free to access.

Do I need prior DSA knowledge?

You should know basic programming constructs (loops, variables, functions) in at least one language. We cover concepts from scratch, but it ramps up quickly.

What languages are supported?

You can solve problems in any language (C++, Java, Python). The logic and patterns remain the same.
